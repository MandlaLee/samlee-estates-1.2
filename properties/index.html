What to Look for in Student Accommodation

Students and parents should compare accommodation carefully before applying.

Safe Environment

Choose accommodation with clear access rules, safety awareness and responsible management.

Study-Friendly Space

Your accommodation should make it easier to focus, rest and follow an academic routine.

Campus Access

Accommodation near campus or transport routes can reduce daily pressure.

Clear Costs

Understand rent, WiFi, electricity, water, transport and other expenses before moving in.
