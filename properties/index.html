Property FAQs

Questions Before Choosing a Property

Quick answers about availability, facilities, NSFAS accommodation and applying for student accommodation.

Are rooms currently available?
Availability can change during busy periods. Apply online or contact SamLee Estates on WhatsApp to ask about the latest room status.
Should I apply early?
Yes. Students should apply early because accommodation demand increases before registration season.
What facilities should I ask about?
Ask about WiFi, room furniture, kitchen access, laundry, bathroom access, electricity, water, safety and house rules.
Are the rooms furnished?
Room setup may differ by property. Confirm what is included before finalising accommodation.
Is SamLee Estates NSFAS accredited?
Yes. SamLee Estates provides NSFAS accredited student accommodation in Bloemfontein.
Can NSFAS students apply for these properties?
Yes. NSFAS-funded students can apply online and select NSFAS as their funding type.
Can I book a viewing?
Yes. Use the WhatsApp booking link or contact SamLee Estates to ask about viewing arrangements.
Can parents attend a viewing?
Yes. Parents and guardians can ask questions about safety, location, costs, house rules and facilities.
