Facilities FAQs

Questions About Student Accommodation Facilities

Quick answers about WiFi, furniture, safety, shared spaces and what students should ask before moving in.

Is WiFi important in student accommodation?
Yes. WiFi helps students with assignments, research, online learning, communication and student administration.
Should I ask if WiFi is included?
Yes. Always ask whether WiFi is included, shared, limited or charged separately.
Should I ask if the room is furnished?
Yes. Ask whether the room includes a bed, desk, chair, wardrobe and storage space.
What makes a room study-friendly?
A study-friendly room should have enough space, suitable lighting, desk space and a quieter environment.
What shared facilities should I ask about?
Ask about shared kitchens, bathrooms, laundry access, cleaning expectations and house rules.
Should I ask about bathroom sharing?
Yes. Ask how many students share a bathroom and how cleaning is handled.
What safety facilities should I ask about?
Ask about locks, access control, lighting, visitor rules, emergency contacts and reporting procedures.
Are house rules important?
Yes. House rules help manage visitors, noise, cleaning, shared spaces and student conduct.
What should parents ask about facilities?
Parents should ask about safety, WiFi, furniture, kitchen access, bathrooms, laundry, transport and total monthly cost.
Can parents contact SamLee Estates?
Yes. Parents and guardians can contact SamLee Estates about accommodation, safety, location and applications.
