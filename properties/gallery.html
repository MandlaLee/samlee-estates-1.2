Frequently Asked Questions

Can I view student accommodation photos before applying?
Yes. Students and parents should review available photos, ask questions and confirm important accommodation details before making decisions.
What should I check in accommodation photos?
Check room setup, study space, safety features, shared areas, kitchen access, bathroom access, laundry arrangements and general property condition.
Should parents view the gallery?
Yes. Parents can use the gallery to understand the accommodation environment and prepare questions about safety, location, facilities and house rules.
Can NSFAS students apply for SamLee Estates accommodation?
Yes. SamLee Estates provides NSFAS accredited student accommodation in Bloemfontein.
Does SamLee Estates offer accommodation near UFS and CUT?
Yes. SamLee Estates provides student accommodation options for students studying near UFS and CUT.
How do I apply after viewing the gallery?
Use the online application page and submit your contact details, institution, funding type, preferred area and move-in date.
