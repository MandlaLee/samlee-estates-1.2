Questions About Viewing Accommodation Photos

Quick answers for students and parents reviewing accommodation photos before applying.

Can I view photos before applying?
Yes. Students and parents should review available photos, ask questions and confirm important details before finalising accommodation.
What should I check in accommodation photos?
Check room setup, study space, safety features, shared areas, kitchen access, bathroom access, laundry arrangements and general property condition.
Should I choose a room only because it looks nice?
No. The room should also be practical, safe, study-friendly and suitable for your budget and daily routine.
Should I ask what furniture is included?
Yes. Always ask whether the room includes a bed, desk, chair, wardrobe or storage space.
Should parents view the gallery?
Yes. Parents can use the gallery to understand the accommodation environment and prepare questions about safety, location, facilities and house rules.
What should parents ask after viewing photos?
Parents should ask about safety, access rules, costs, what is included, transport, emergency contacts and the move-in process.
Can I book a viewing after seeing the gallery?
Yes. Use the WhatsApp viewing link or contact SamLee Estates to ask about viewing arrangements.
Can parents attend the viewing?
Yes. Parents and guardians can ask questions about safety, location, facilities, costs and house rules.
