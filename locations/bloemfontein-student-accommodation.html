Discover quality student accommodation near UFS and CUT in Bloemfontein's most popular student suburbs.

Why Study in Bloemfontein?

Bloemfontein is one of South Africa's leading university cities and attracts thousands of students every year. Home to the University of the Free State (UFS), the Central University of Technology (CUT), TVET colleges and private institutions, the city offers students a vibrant academic environment combined with an affordable cost of living.

Students choose Bloemfontein because it provides excellent educational opportunities, manageable living costs, strong transport infrastructure and a welcoming student community. The city's central location and student-friendly atmosphere make it one of the most popular destinations for higher education in South Africa.

Finding the right accommodation is one of the most important decisions a student can make. The right location can improve academic performance, reduce travel costs and create a better university experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which area is best for student accommodation in Bloemfontein?
Popular student areas include Universitas, Park West, Willows, Brandwag and Wesdene. The best choice depends on your institution, budget and personal preferences.
Is Bloemfontein a good student city?
Yes. Bloemfontein offers quality universities, affordable living costs and a strong student community.
Where do most UFS students stay?
Many UFS students choose accommodation in Universitas, Brandwag, Park West and other nearby suburbs.
Where do CUT students stay?
CUT students often choose accommodation in Willows, Wesdene and other centrally located areas.
How early should I apply for accommodation?
Students should begin their accommodation search as early as possible because demand increases significantly before each academic year.
What should I look for when choosing accommodation?
Students should consider location, security, WiFi, study facilities, affordability and access to campus.
