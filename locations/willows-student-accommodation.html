Student Accommodation in Willows

Affordable, secure and centrally located student accommodation in one of Bloemfontein's most established student suburbs.

Why Students Choose Willows

Willows is one of Bloemfontein's best-known student accommodation areas. Its central location provides convenient access to the University of the Free State, the Central University of Technology and several colleges throughout the city. Students living in Willows benefit from being close to transport routes, shopping facilities, restaurants, entertainment venues and academic institutions. The area offers a balance between affordability and convenience, making it attractive to students who want to stay connected to both campus and city life. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Close to UFS and CUT

One of the biggest advantages of living in Willows is its accessibility. Students can easily travel to the University of the Free State and the Central University of Technology while remaining close to the Bloemfontein CBD. Many student residences in the area specifically market themselves to students attending these institutions. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

This convenient location helps students reduce travel costs and spend more time focusing on academics, student activities and personal development.

A True Student Community

Willows has developed into a student-focused suburb with numerous residences, student apartment buildings and private accommodation providers operating in the area. Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Willows located?
Willows is a centrally located suburb in Bloemfontein known for its strong student accommodation presence and convenient access to major educational institutions.
Is Willows close to UFS?
Yes. Willows offers convenient access to the University of the Free State and is a popular accommodation area for university students.
Is Willows suitable for CUT students?
Yes. The suburb provides convenient access to the Central University of Technology and several student residences specifically cater to CUT students.
Why do students choose Willows?
Students choose Willows because of its central location, affordability, transport access and strong student community.
Does Willows have student accommodation?
Yes. Willows is home to numerous student accommodation providers, residences and apartment developments serving students from across Bloemfontein.
