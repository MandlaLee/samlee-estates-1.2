Student Accommodation in Universitas

Live in the heart of Bloemfontein's student community with accommodation conveniently located near the University of the Free State.

Why Universitas Is the Most Popular Student Suburb Near UFS

Universitas is widely regarded as the student accommodation capital of Bloemfontein. Located directly adjacent to the University of the Free State, the suburb has become home to thousands of students who want the convenience of living close to campus while enjoying a vibrant student community.

Many of the city's student residences, private accommodation providers and purpose-built student housing developments are located in Universitas. The suburb offers a balance between academic convenience, affordability and lifestyle opportunities that make it attractive to both local and international students.

Students living in Universitas often enjoy shorter travel times to lectures, easier access to university facilities and a stronger connection to campus life compared to students living in more distant suburbs. Close to the University of the Free State

One of the biggest advantages of living in Universitas is proximity to campus. Many student residences are located within walking distance of UFS, allowing students to attend lectures, study sessions and extracurricular activities without relying heavily on transport. Several accommodation providers in the area highlight locations less than one kilometre from campus. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Living close to campus helps students save money on transportation while giving them more time for academics, sports and social activities.

Student Lifestyle in Universitas

Universitas is built around student life. The suburb offers easy access to restaurants, takeaways, grocery stores, pharmacies, gyms and other services commonly used by students. Accommodation providers frequently market the area's convenience and student-focused environment. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Because so many students live in the area, Universitas has developed a unique community atmosphere where students can build friendships, collaborate academically and participate in university life.

Safety and Security

Safety remains one of the most important considerations when choosing accommodation. Students and parents often prioritise accommodation with access control, secure boundaries and clear management policies.

Who Should Consider Universitas?

Universitas is an excellent choice for:

  • First-year students attending UFS
  • Returning undergraduate students
  • Postgraduate students
  • NSFAS-funded students
  • International students
  • Students who prefer walking to campus

The suburb's strong student presence and proximity to university facilities make it one of the most practical accommodation locations in Bloemfontein.
