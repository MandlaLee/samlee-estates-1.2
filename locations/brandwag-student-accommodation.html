Student Accommodation in Brandwag

Live close to UFS, shopping centres and student amenities in one of Bloemfontein's most convenient and sought-after suburbs.

Why Students Choose Brandwag

Brandwag is one of Bloemfontein's most established residential suburbs and has become increasingly popular among university students because of its excellent location. The suburb sits close to the University of the Free State, major transport routes, shopping centres and essential services, making it a practical choice for student living. Students often choose Brandwag because it offers easy access to campus while also placing them near retail centres, restaurants, medical facilities and recreational opportunities. Close to the University of the Free State

One of Brandwag's biggest advantages is its proximity to UFS. Several student accommodation providers in the suburb advertise locations within walking distance of campus, making it easier for students to attend lectures, tutorials and university activities without relying heavily on transport. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Living near campus can reduce transport expenses, save valuable time and help students become more involved in university life. Students often find that proximity to campus improves their ability to balance academics, social activities and personal wellbeing.
