FAQs

Got Questions?

Find quick answers about applications, NSFAS, facilities, payments and student living.

How do I apply for accommodation?
Complete the online application form with your student details, institution, funding type, preferred area and move-in date.
What happens after I apply?
SamLee Estates receives your application details and can contact you about availability or next steps.
Is SamLee Estates NSFAS accredited?
Yes. SamLee Estates provides NSFAS accredited student accommodation in Bloemfontein.
Can NSFAS students apply online?
Yes. NSFAS-funded students can apply online and select NSFAS as their funding type.
What facilities should students ask about?
Ask about WiFi, furniture, kitchen access, bathroom access, laundry, safety, study space and house rules.
Is WiFi important?
Yes. WiFi supports online learning, research, communication and student administration.
Can parents contact SamLee Estates?
Yes. Parents and guardians can contact SamLee Estates about safety, location, applications and student support.
Should students read house rules?
Yes. House rules explain visitors, noise, shared spaces, cleaning and expected conduct.
What costs should students budget for?
Students should budget for rent, food, transport, toiletries, laundry, printing, data and emergencies.
Should I ask what is included?
Yes. Always ask what is included in rent and what is paid separately before moving in.
