Most Common Questions

What is SamLee Estates?
SamLee Estates is a student accommodation provider in Bloemfontein focused on student housing near UFS and CUT.
Where is SamLee Estates based?
SamLee Estates is based in Bloemfontein, Free State, and serves students studying in the city.
Does SamLee Estates offer accommodation near UFS?
Yes. SamLee Estates provides student accommodation options for students studying near the University of the Free State.
Does SamLee Estates offer accommodation near CUT?
Yes. SamLee Estates provides student accommodation options for students studying near the Central University of Technology.
Is SamLee Estates NSFAS accredited?
Yes. SamLee Estates provides NSFAS accredited student accommodation in Bloemfontein.
How do I apply for accommodation?
You can apply online through the SamLee Estates application page by submitting your name, contact details, institution, funding type, preferred area and move-in date.
Should I apply early?
Yes. Students should apply as early as possible before registration season because accommodation demand increases before the academic year begins.
Can parents contact SamLee Estates?
Yes. Parents and guardians can contact SamLee Estates to ask about location, safety, house rules, applications and student accommodation support.

Quick Answers

What areas does SamLee Estates serve?
SamLee Estates focuses on student accommodation in Bloemfontein, especially areas connected to UFS, CUT and nearby student communities.
Can first-year students apply?
Yes. First-year students are encouraged to apply early so they can prepare properly before registration and orientation.
Can postgraduate students apply?
Yes. Postgraduate students can apply for student accommodation through SamLee Estates.
Do I need proof of registration?
You may need proof of registration or other supporting documents during the accommodation process, depending on your funding and application situation.
What should I check before choosing accommodation?
Check location, safety, cost, WiFi, house rules, transport access, room type, facilities and whether the accommodation meets your funding requirements.
What if I do not know which property to choose?
You can apply online with your preferred area, campus and funding type. SamLee Estates can contact you about suitable accommodation options.
