Contact Questions

Should I apply online or send a WhatsApp first?
If you are looking for accommodation, apply online first. WhatsApp can be used for follow-up questions, viewing bookings or urgent enquiries.
Can parents contact SamLee Estates?
Yes. Parents and guardians can contact SamLee Estates to ask about student accommodation, safety, location, applications, costs and support.
Which contact should NSFAS students use?
NSFAS-funded students can use the NSFAS accommodation support contact or apply online and select NSFAS as their funding type.
Who should current tenants contact?
Current tenants should contact the caretaker linked to their property or use urgent property support where needed.
Are these final contact numbers?
No. These are placeholder contact numbers. Replace them with the final SamLee Estates WhatsApp and caretaker numbers before launch.
Can I email SamLee Estates?
Yes. General enquiries can be sent to info@samlee.co.za and applications can be sent to applications@samlee.co.za.
