About SamLee Estates

Student accommodation in Bloemfontein created to support students studying near UFS and CUT with safe, convenient and study-friendly housing.

Who We Are

SamLee Estates is a student accommodation provider in Bloemfontein focused on helping students find a place to stay while studying near the University of the Free State and the Central University of Technology.

We understand that accommodation is one of the most important parts of a student’s academic journey. A room is not just a place to sleep. It affects safety, transport, study routine, budgeting, rest and the overall university experience.

Our goal is to provide student accommodation that feels practical, accessible and supportive for students and parents preparing for university life in Bloemfontein.

SamLee Estates forms part of the wider SamLee brand family, with a focus on student housing, accommodation support and student living in Bloemfontein.

Our Focus

We focus on student accommodation near major Bloemfontein institutions, especially UFS and CUT.

We also support students looking for NSFAS accredited accommodation and practical guidance before moving into student housing.

Our Story

Built for Students Preparing for University Life

SamLee Estates was created to serve the real needs of students looking for accommodation in Bloemfontein. Many students search for accommodation under pressure, often close to registration season, without enough guidance about safety, location, cost or requirements.

Our mission is to make the accommodation journey clearer. Students should know where to apply, what to prepare, what to ask and how to choose accommodation that supports their studies.

We are building SamLee Estates as a trusted student accommodation brand for Bloemfontein, with strong focus on UFS, CUT, NSFAS accommodation support and student living guidance.
