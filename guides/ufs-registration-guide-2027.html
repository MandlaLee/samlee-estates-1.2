UFS Registration Guide for 2027 Students

Registration is one of the most important steps after receiving admission to the University of the Free State. It is the process that officially connects you to your qualification, modules, timetable and academic year.

The UFS uses an online registration platform for both new and returning students, with assisted online registration available on campus for students who need additional help. Students who need on-campus assistance are usually required to show their offer letter to access campus support.

This guide is written to help 2027 students understand what to prepare before registration, how online registration works and why accommodation should be secured early. Always confirm final dates, fees and official instructions directly on the UFS registration website before taking action.

Important: UFS registration dates and processes can change each academic year. Always confirm final 2027 registration instructions through official UFS communication and the UFS registration page.

Step 1: Confirm Your Admission Status

Before registration, students should make sure their admission has been confirmed and that they understand any outstanding conditions attached to their offer.

  • Check your admission outcome.
  • Confirm your student number.
  • Read your offer letter carefully.
  • Confirm your campus and qualification.
  • Check whether any documents are still outstanding.
  • Review faculty-specific requirements.

UFS online applications for 2027 opened on 1 April 2026, and UFS states that application to the university is free. Students preparing for 2027 should use official UFS application and registration channels for final instructions.

Step 3: Complete Your Online Registration Task

UFS directs students to complete registration through the online registration platform. For first-year students, UFS explains that students should log into PeopleSoft to complete their registration and accept the terms and conditions. UFS also notes that students should submit their online registration request even if the first payment has not yet been made, and that finalisation happens once all conditions are met.

During online registration, students may need to confirm:

  • Personal details
  • Qualification information
  • Campus
  • Modules
  • Terms and conditions
  • Financial arrangements
  • Contact information

Step 5: Plan Your Accommodation Early

Accommodation is one of the biggest decisions students make before arriving in Bloemfontein. Students who wait until registration week often face limited options, higher pressure and less time to compare locations.

If you are studying at UFS, accommodation near campus can help reduce travel time, lower transport costs and make it easier to attend lectures, access libraries and participate in student life.

UFS First-Year Registration Tips

First-year students should treat registration as more than an admin task. It is the beginning of university life. A smooth registration process helps students settle faster, attend orientation, receive their timetable and prepare for lectures.

  • Do not wait until the last week to prepare.
  • Check your UFS login details early.
  • Read all official UFS communication carefully.
  • Confirm your qualification and campus before registering.
  • Prepare accommodation before registration pressure begins.
  • Download your proof of registration after completing the process.
  • Save copies of important documents.
  • Ask for help early if something does not work.

UFS Registration and NSFAS Students

NSFAS-funded students should ensure that their funding, registration and accommodation processes align. Accommodation should be handled early because students often need proof of registration, lease information and other supporting documents during the accommodation process.
