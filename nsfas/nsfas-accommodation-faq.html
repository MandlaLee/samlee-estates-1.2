NSFAS Accommodation FAQ

Answers to common questions about NSFAS accredited student accommodation, applications, requirements, allowances and accommodation near UFS and CUT.

General NSFAS Accommodation Questions

What is NSFAS accommodation?
NSFAS accommodation refers to student housing support available to qualifying NSFAS-funded students while studying at an approved public institution.
Is SamLee Estates NSFAS accredited?
Yes. SamLee Estates provides NSFAS accredited student accommodation in Bloemfontein.
Can NSFAS students stay in private accommodation?
Yes. NSFAS-funded students may stay in approved or accredited private accommodation, subject to the required processes and institutional rules.
Why does accreditation matter?
Accreditation helps ensure that student accommodation meets required standards for safety, suitability and student living.
Does NSFAS pay for any accommodation?
Students should use approved or accredited accommodation and follow the correct application process to qualify for accommodation support.

Application Questions

How do I apply for NSFAS accommodation?
Students usually need NSFAS funding approval, institutional registration and an approved accommodation application before moving in.
When should I apply for accommodation?
Apply as early as possible. Student accommodation demand increases strongly before registration and the start of the academic year.
Can first-year students apply?
Yes. First-year students are encouraged to apply early so they can secure accommodation before orientation and registration.
Do postgraduate students qualify?
Postgraduate students may apply for accommodation, depending on their funding, registration and accommodation eligibility.
Do I need a lease agreement?
Yes. A valid lease agreement is usually required as part of the accommodation process.

Does SamLee Estates offer NSFAS accommodation near CUT?

Popular student areas include Universitas, Park West, Willows, Brandwag and Wesdene.
Is it better to live close to campus?
Living close to campus can reduce travel time, lower transport costs and improve access to lectures, libraries and student facilities.

Allowance and Payment Questions

How much is the NSFAS accommodation allowance?
Allowance amounts may change each academic year. Students should confirm the latest amount through official NSFAS and university communication.
Can I receive both accommodation and transport allowance?
Students generally cannot receive both for the same period. If you live in approved accommodation, you may fall under accommodation support instead of transport support.
Does NSFAS pay the student or the accommodation provider?
Payment processes depend on the institution, NSFAS systems and accommodation arrangements. Students should confirm the process during application.
What happens if my accommodation is not approved?
Students may need to choose approved accommodation or follow the required appeal or correction process through the relevant institution or NSFAS platform.

Facilities and Safety Questions

Is WiFi important for NSFAS accommodation?
Yes. Reliable internet access is important for online learning, assignments, research and communication.
Should student accommodation have security?
Yes. Students and parents should prioritise accommodation with appropriate safety and security measures.
What facilities should student accommodation provide?
Students should look for safe rooms, study-friendly spaces, sanitation, internet access, laundry access and a convenient location.
Are visitors allowed?
Visitor rules depend on the accommodation provider’s house rules. Students should review the visitor policy before moving in.
