NSFAS Accommodation Allowance Guide 2027

Understand how NSFAS accommodation funding works, what students should prepare, and how to secure accredited student accommodation before the academic year begins.

What Is the NSFAS Accommodation Allowance?

The NSFAS accommodation allowance is financial support intended to help qualifying funded students pay for approved student accommodation while studying at a public university or TVET college.

Accommodation funding is important because many students cannot study effectively if they do not have safe, stable and suitable housing near their institution.

For 2027, students should always confirm the latest official allowance amounts and payment rules directly from NSFAS and their institution before signing accommodation agreements.

Important: NSFAS allowance amounts and processes can change each academic year. Students should confirm the latest 2027 figures through official NSFAS and university communication channels.

How NSFAS Accommodation Funding Usually Works

Funding Approval

Students must first qualify for NSFAS funding.

Registration

Students must be registered at a recognised public institution.

Accredited Housing

Accommodation must follow the required approval and accreditation process.

Lease Agreement

A valid signed lease is usually required for accommodation processing.

Who May Qualify for the Accommodation Allowance?

A student may generally need to meet funding, registration and accommodation requirements before receiving accommodation support. The exact requirements depend on NSFAS rules, institutional processes and the accommodation approval system for that year.

  • The student should be NSFAS-funded.
  • The student should be registered at a public university or TVET college.
  • The accommodation should be accredited or approved through the relevant process.
  • The student should not be receiving a transport allowance for the same period.
  • The student may need a valid lease agreement.
  • The student should follow the institution’s accommodation application process.

Accommodation Allowance vs Transport Allowance

Students usually cannot receive both accommodation and transport support for the same period. If a student stays at home and travels to campus, they may fall under transport support instead. If a student stays in approved accommodation, they may fall under accommodation support.

This is why students must choose carefully and follow the correct application process at their institution.
