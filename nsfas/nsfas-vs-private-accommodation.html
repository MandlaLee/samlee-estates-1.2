NSFAS vs Private Accommodation

Compare funding, payment, flexibility and responsibility before choosing accommodation.

How to compare options

NSFAS-funded and private accommodation can both work, but students must understand what is paid, when it is paid and what responsibilities remain with the student or guardian.

  • Compare total monthly cost, not only rent.
  • Check whether WiFi, water, electricity and laundry are included.
  • Understand contract length and cancellation rules.
  • Ask whether the accommodation supports NSFAS or bursary documentation.
